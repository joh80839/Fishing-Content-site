## Project Proposal

**Project Description:** Rod and Reel Combos are considered the most beginner friendly tools to catch fish with. This website describes three rod and reel combos that are under $50 for beginners. Since a rod and reel under $50 is considered non-expensive in today's market, this will help people who are new to fishing and have a low budget to spend on fishing equipment.

**Feature Plan:** 
- Project 01 features: There will be a title page with pictures and a clickable link to get to the second page that will eventually list the Rod and Reel Combos.
- Project 02 features: There will be a title page with pictures and a clickable link to get to the second page that has details and pictures about the rod and reel combos.
